When do I need to leave?

Working backwards to figure out what time or leave, or wake up, is familiar to all of us.

Flight leaves at 9:00, so be at the gate by 8:30, leave 20 minutes to get through the terminal, 20 for TSA, 15 to park and take shuttle, 45 minute drive..."

The calculator below will do all that reverse clock math for you:

  1. Enter your target end time (24-hour format)
  2. Add the duration in minutes for intermediate steps (press Enter to insert rows)
  3. Your start time is calculated
  4. Optionally bookmark the permalink URL as a template for your next time (example)
